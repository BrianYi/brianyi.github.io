Expectation Value

  • intro: The expectation value of a function f(x)f(x)f(x) in a variable xxx is denoted ⟨f(x)⟩\lang{f(x)\rang}⟨f(x)⟩ or E{f(x)}E\{f(x)\}E{f(x)}.
  • single discrete variable:

    ⟨f(x)⟩=∑xf(x)P(x)\begin{aligned} \lang{f(x)}\rang=\sum_{x}{f(x)P(x)} \end{aligned}⟨f(x)⟩=x∑​f(x)P(x)​

    P(x)P(x)P(x) is probability density function(概率密度函数).
  • single continuous variable:

    ⟨f(x)⟩=∫f(x)P(x)dx\begin{aligned} \lang{f(x)}\rang=\int{f(x)P(x)dx} \end{aligned}⟨f(x)⟩=∫f(x)P(x)dx​

  • multiple discrete variables:

    ⟨f(x1,...,xn)⟩=∑x1,...,xnf(x1,...,xn)P(x1,...,xn)\begin{aligned} \lang{f(x_1,...,x_n)}\rang=\sum_{x_1,...,x_n}{f(x_1,...,x_n)P(x_1,...,x_n)} \end{aligned}⟨f(x1​,...,xn​)⟩=x1​,...,xn​∑​f(x1​,...,xn​)P(x1​,...,xn​)​

  • multiple continuous variables:

    ⟨f(x1,...,xn)⟩=∫f(x1,...,xn)P(x1,...,xn)dx1,...,xn\begin{aligned} \lang{f(x_1,...,x_n)}\rang=\int{f(x_1,...,x_n)}P(x_1,...,x_n)d{x_1,...,x_n} \end{aligned}⟨f(x1​,...,xn​)⟩=∫f(x1​,...,xn​)P(x1​,...,xn​)dx1​,...,xn​​

Variance

  • intro: For a single variate XXX having a distribution P(x)P(x)P(x) with known population mean(总体均值) μ\muμ, the population variance(总体方差) var(X)var(X)var(X), commonly also written σ2\sigma^2σ2, is defined as

    σ2≡⟨(X−μ)2⟩\begin{aligned} \sigma^2\equiv\lang(X-\mu)^2\rang \end{aligned}σ2≡⟨(X−μ)2⟩​

    where μ\muμ is the population mean(总体均值) and ⟨X⟩\lang{X}\rang⟨X⟩ denotes the expectation value(期望值) of XXX.
  • discrete distribution(离散分布):

    σ2=∑i=1NP(xi)(xi−μ)2\begin{aligned} \sigma^2=\sum_{i=1}^N{P(x_i)(x_i-\mu)^2} \end{aligned}σ2=i=1∑N​P(xi​)(xi​−μ)2​

  • continuous distribution(连续分布):

    σ2=∫P(x)(x−μ)2dx\begin{aligned} \sigma^2=\int{P(x)(x-\mu)^2}dx \end{aligned}σ2=∫P(x)(x−μ)2dx​

    The variance is therefore equal to the second central moment μ2\mu_2μ2​.
    If the underlying distribution is not known, then the sample variance may be computed as:

    sN2≡1N∑i=1N(xi−xˉ)2\begin{aligned} s_N^2\equiv{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i-\bar{x})^2} \end{aligned}sN2​≡N1​i=1∑N​(xi​−xˉ)2​

    where xˉ\bar{x}xˉ is the sample mean.
    Note that the sample variance sN2s_N^2sN2​ defined above is not an unbiased estimator for the population variance σ2\sigma^2σ2. In order to obtain an unbiased estimator for σ2\sigma^2σ2, it is necessary to instead define a “bias-corrected sample variance”

    sN−12≡1N−1∑i=1N(xi−xˉ)2\begin{aligned} s_{N-1}^2\equiv{\frac{1}{N-1}\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i-\bar{x})^2} \end{aligned}sN−12​≡N−11​i=1∑N​(xi​−xˉ)2​

    The distinction between sN2s_N^2sN2​ and sN−12s_{N-1}^2sN−12​ is a common source of confusion, and extreme care should be exercised when consulting the literature to determine which convention is in use, especially since the uninformative notation sss is commonly used for both.
    The square root of the variance is known as the standard deviation.

standard deviation

  • intro: a measure that is used to quantify the amount of variation or dispersion of a set of data values. A low standard deviation indicates that the data points tend to be close to the mean (also called the expected value) of the set, while a high standard deviation indicates that the data points are spread out over a wider range of values.
    σ\sigmaσ: population standard deviation
    sss: sample standard deviation
    • sample standard deviation(样品标准偏差):
      x1,x2,...,xN{x_1,x_2,...,x_N}x1​,x2​,...,xN​ are the observed valued of the sample items,xˉ\bar{x}xˉ is the mean value of these observations, N is the number of observations in the sample.

      s=1N−1∑i=1N(xi−xˉ)2\begin{aligned} s=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N-1}\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i-\bar{x})^2} \end{aligned}s=N−11​i=1∑N​(xi​−xˉ)2​​

    • population standard deviation(总体标准偏差):

      σ=1N∑i=1N(xi−μ)2μ=1N∑i=1Nxi\begin{aligned} &\sigma=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i-\mu)^2}\\ &\mu=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^Nx_i \end{aligned}​σ=N1​i=1∑N​(xi​−μ)2​μ=N1​i=1∑N​xi​​

    • unbiased and biased estimator The simplest possible normalization is 1n\frac{1}{n}n1​. However, this provides a biased estimation of variance particularly for small n. The proper normalization for an unbiased estimator is 1n−1\frac{1}{n-1}n−11​.
    • mean deviation(平均偏差):

      Mean Deviation=1N∑i=1N∣xi−μ∣\begin{aligned} Mean\ Deviation=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N|x_i-\mu| \end{aligned}Mean Deviation=N1​i=1∑N​∣xi​−μ∣​

    • zero mean(零均值化):

      Zero Mean=xi−xˉ\begin{aligned} Zero\ Mean=x_i-\bar{x} \end{aligned}Zero Mean=xi​−xˉ​

      用处:
      随机变量A={a1,a2,...,an},B={b1,b2,...,bn}A=\{a_1,a_2,...,a_n\},B=\{b_1,b_2,...,b_n\}A={a1​,a2​,...,an​},B={b1​,b2​,...,bn​},都已进行零均值化.
      则求方差,协方差

      σA2=⟨aiai⟩i=E{aiai}σAB2=⟨aibi⟩i=E{aibi}\begin{aligned} &\sigma_A^2=\lang{a_ia_i}\rang_i=E\{a_ia_i\}\\ &\sigma_{AB}^2=\lang{a_ib_i}\rang_i=E\{a_ib_i\}\\ \end{aligned}​σA2​=⟨ai​ai​⟩i​=E{ai​ai​}σAB2​=⟨ai​bi​⟩i​=E{ai​bi​}​
